Coleslaw Dressing

Desciption

This dressing features a rich, creamy texture with a perfect blend of sweetness and tanginess, complemented by a subtle peppery kick. It's a versatile addition to your kitchen, ideal for salads, sandwiches, and various dishes, adding a delightful burst of flavor with every drizzle.

Ingredients

  • ½ cup mayonnaise
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 1 ½ tablespoons lemon juice
  • 1 tablespoon vinegar
  • ½ teaspoon ground black pepper
  • ¼ teaspoon salt

Steps

  1. Gather all the Ingredients
  2. Whisk mayonnaise, sugar, lemon juice, vinegar, pepper, and salt together in a bowl until smooth and creamy.
  3. Store in an airtight container.
