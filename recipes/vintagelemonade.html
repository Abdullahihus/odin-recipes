Vintage lemonade

Desciption

This vintage lemonade recipe offers a refreshing taste of nostalgia, capturing the essence of classic, old-fashioned lemonade. Made the traditional way, it boasts a perfect balance of tart and sweet flavors, creating a thirst-quenching beverage that's ideal for hot summer days. With its bright, citrusy notes and natural sweetness, this lemonade will transport you back to simpler times, evoking memories of sunny afternoons and carefree moments. Perfect for picnics, gatherings, or simply savoring at home, this vintage lemonade is a timeless treat for all ages.

Ingredients

  • 5 Lemons
  • 1 ¼ cups of white sugar
  • 1 ¼ quarts water

Steps

  1. Peel the rinds from the 5 lemons and cut them into 1/2 inch slices. Set the lemons aside.
  2. Place the rinds in a bowl and sprinkle the sugar over them. Let this stand for about one hour, so that the sugar begins to soak up the oils from the lemons.
  3. Bring water to a boil in a covered saucepan and then pour the hot water over the sugared lemon rinds. Allow this mixture to cool for 20 minutes and then remove the rinds.
  4. Squeeze the lemons into another bowl. Pour the juice through a strainer into the sugar mixture. Stir well, pour into pitcher and pop it in the fridge! Serve with ice cubes.
